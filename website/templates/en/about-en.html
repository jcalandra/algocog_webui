About :

Joséphine Calandra is a PhD student co-supervised in musicology and computer science. She's supervised within the Musicology Research Institute (IReMus) by Jean-Marc Chouvel, and within the Laboratory of Research in Computer Science of Bordeaux (LaBRI) by Myriam Desainte-Catherine. Joséphine is a Computer Science Engineer graduated in 2019 at ENSEIRB-MATMECA, and she is currently working on a thesis on cognitive algorithmics and its musical applications : real-time structuration at different hierarchical levels of music and the effects that it involves on the cognitive system.

This thesis is funded as part of the 80|Prime call for projects by the CNRS.

The different actors are the CNRS , IReMus , Sorbonne University, DS433 Concepts and Languages doctoral school, le LaBRI , Bordeaux University and SCRIME.
